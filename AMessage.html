A Message from
Henrico Silvis

Dear Shareholders,

In business and investing, success often comes down to how well we recognise and act on opportunities. The best opportunities rarely arrive dressed in certainty — they come disguised in moments of difficulty, volatility, and doubt. Those who have the courage to lean in when others hesitate are the ones who capture the greatest rewards.

"When others are fearful, be greedy. The short-term is a voting machine; the long-term is a weighing machine."

One of the most important things I want to highlight is the nature of business itself. Businesses are inherently volatile. In the short term, markets behave like voting machines — sentiment, trends, and emotion drive prices in ways that often have little to do with a company's true value. But over the long term — 7 years or more — markets act as weighing machines. They ultimately measure the real strength, performance, and enduring worth of a business.

Key Principle
The best thing you can do in a down cycle is accumulate shares in exceptional businesses. History has proven, time and again, that downturns create the greatest opportunities for long-term wealth creation. Down cycles are where the real money is made.

When prices fall, the disciplined investor's response is clear: buy more. Not to be contrarian for its own sake, but because quality businesses purchased at dislocated prices are the seeds of extraordinary long-term returns. The 2008 financial crisis, the COVID-19 pandemic crash — both rewarded those who stayed the course and leaned in.

At Silvis Investment Journey, I remain committed to looking beyond short-term noise and focusing on long-term fundamentals. Every decision is guided by the belief that enduring value creation is built over years, not days. I own businesses with dominant market positions, high barriers to entry, and cash-rich balance sheets — compounding machines that reward patience.

Thank you for your continued trust and partnership. Staying the course is not always comfortable, but it is, in our conviction, the surest path to exceptional outcomes.

Henrico Silvis
Managing Partner
