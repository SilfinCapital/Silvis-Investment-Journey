Personal Investment Portfolio

About
Us

The Silvis Investment Journey is a personal portfolio built on disciplined, long-term investing. Patient, value-oriented decisions — made with conviction and a focus on downside protection — deliver superior outcomes over time.

This is a concentrated personal portfolio combining careful security selection with pragmatic risk management, guided by my commitment to transparency and long-term thinking.

The Silfin Partnership holds a concentrated portfolio of the highest-quality businesses in the world — monopolies, gatekeepers, and toll booths — purchased at reasonable valuations and held for the long term.

Investment Philosophy

How we
think & invest

01
Long Horizon
I invest with a multi-year view. Markets are noisy in the short run but reveal true value over time. Our recommended investment horizon is 7+ years.
02
Value Discipline
I prioritise investments where the balance of risk and reward is attractive — especially where market volatility creates opportunities to buy quality at a discount.
03
Concentration with Conviction
A focused portfolio of high-conviction ideas rather than broad, undifferentiated exposure. Concentration in great businesses is not risk — it is insight.
04
Capital Preservation
Protecting downside is as important as capturing upside. I use position sizing and active monitoring to manage risk without sacrificing long-term returns.
FAQ

Frequently asked
questions

What does the portfolio invest in?
The portfolio is concentrated in high-quality global equities — primarily listed shares in developed markets. This includes direct holdings in individual companies as well as low-cost index ETFs where appropriate.
What is the minimum investment horizon?
A minimum horizon of 7 years is strongly recommended. The portfolio invests in long-term compounding businesses, and short-term performance is not the objective. The full power of compounding is only realised over multi-year periods.
How is performance measured?
Performance is benchmarked against the MSCI ACWI and a 10% per annum hurdle rate. Results are reported quarterly in the Minimum Disclosure Documents available on the Reports page.
How often are reports published?
Quarterly reports are published within a few weeks of each quarter-end, covering performance, portfolio changes, and commentary on the market environment.
Risk Disclosure
All investing involves risk. Past performance is not a guarantee of future results. Market risk and volatility can cause short-term losses. This portfolio is concentrated in global equities and is expected to exhibit higher volatility than a diversified index.
